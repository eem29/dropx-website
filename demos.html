What we do

A skydiver landing in your event doesn't just open the show, it makes it.

Custom flags, sponsor banners, or the New Zealand flag delivered into stadiums, waterfront events, and civic openings. We work with event organisers to plan the approach, the timing, and the moment. Professional, reliable, and built to be the highlight of the day.

What we deliver

From flags to full productions.

We jump with whatever you need. Custom sponsor banners, national flags, and oversized displays into the heart of your event. From Super Rugby to waterfront festivals, we plan every jump around your event schedule and landing zone.

We work directly with event producers to handle the approach, timing, and airspace coordination. You don't need to know anything about skydiving. We take care of it.
