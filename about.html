Who We Are

Built for those who Want more.

Our Story

Built for those who want more.

Skydive DropX is built for those who want more than just a skydive. It's for people chasing progression, and unforgettable life experiences.

The Vision

A drop zone where everyone feels at home.

Founded by passionate skydivers, DropX exists to create a high energy, community driven drop zone where first timers, students, and experienced jumpers all feel at home.

Whether you're stepping out of an aircraft for the first time or pushing your limits as a seasoned jumper, our focus is simple: deliver a safe, professional, and fun environment, skydiving over Christchurch.

What We Do

Student training. Display skydiving. Sport progression.

We specialise in student training, display skydiving at events, and supporting sport jumpers with coaching and progression pathways. Our team is made up of experienced instructors and pilots who live and breathe aviation and are committed to helping you get the most out of every jump.

It's not just about the jump.

It's about the people, the energy on the ground, and the stories you take home with you.

We're here to build something different. A drop zone that pushes and supports progression, and keeps you coming back for more.
