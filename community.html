Who we are

A sport drop zone built around community.

Skydive DropX is a sport and club drop zone. There is no booking system, no tourist operation, and no front counter. Jump days are organised by the people who jump here, and coordination happens through our Facebook Messenger group.

If you are a licensed jumper looking for a home drop zone, or just want to get some jumps in when visiting Canterbury, this is where you find the crew.

How it works

Simple as that.

1

Join the Messenger group

That is where all jump day coordination happens. Upcoming sessions, weather calls, and who is jumping this weekend.

2

Show up on jump day

Bring your licence, your gear, and your logbook. Grab a ticket and get in the air. NZ$65 per jump from 12,000ft.

3

Jump with the crew

All disciplines welcome. Fun jumping, freefly, canopy work, big ways. If you want coaching, reach out in the group.
